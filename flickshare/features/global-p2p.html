How to Use Global P2P Remote Sharing

1

Open Remote Mode

Launch FlickShare on your Windows PC or Android mobile, and select the 'Remote Sharing' tab.

2

Generate Handshake Pin

Click 'Generate PIN' to create a unique 4-digit temporary passcode for your session connection.

3

Share Pin Code

Send this 4-digit code to the recipient. They will enter it on their FlickShare app to pair securely.

4

Stream Encrypted

Select your folders, pictures, or videos and click send. They stream encrypted directly to their device!
