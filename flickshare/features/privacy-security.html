🛡️ Absolute Privacy & Safety

100% Serverless. Absolute Privacy.

Your data belongs solely to you! FlickShare collects no personal metadata, coordinates, or file contents. All transfers flow directly via encrypted peer-to-peer tunnels.

🛡️

Serverless Architecture Verified

We do not store your files on any cloud servers. Files remain encrypted and flow strictly between your selected sender and receiver devices.

🚫 Zero Data Logging

We never log your personal files, track transfer histories, or track location coordinates. FlickShare operates in complete anonymity, ensuring maximum privacy.

🌐 Secure WebRTC Tunnels

For remote transfers, our signaling server is only used to establish the initial peer handshake. Once paired, files are piped through a secure, encrypted WebRTC tunnel directly.

👶 Families Policy Compliant (AdMob)

Our Android package is fully compliant with Google Play Console's strict Family Policy. All displayed banner/interstitial advertisements are child-safe (G/PG-rated ratings).

🔐 Zero Unnecessary Permissions

FlickShare never requests unnecessary permissions like your contacts list, text messages, or call histories. We only request files access and local network permissions required for transfer.

Our Privacy Protection Lifecycle

1

Direct Handshake

FlickShare sets up direct local socket links or WebRTC E2EE signaling handshakes immediately.

2

Serverless Stream

Files flow directly from sender storage to receiver storage. No cloud database ever holds your data.

3

In accordance with Families policy, all ads are G/PG filtered, blocking malware and pop-ups.

Complete Anonymity

Zero tracking logs, coordinates, or transfers list records are stored. Complete privacy by design.
